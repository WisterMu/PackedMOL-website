War Never Changes...

Good thing that we do!

I'm a bit new to the Fallout franchise, but after watching the Fallout TV Show on Amazon Prime, I really wanted to try the games out. I ended up buying the game in March 2026 during the Steam spring sale, so here's me finally documenting my playthrough! I do know the basic lore and surface level info on each faction, but I'm excited to learn more about each one.

Meet Jackie

A courier who travels the Mojave desert, she was shot and left for dead by a man in a checkered suit. After being saved by Viktor and Doc Mitchell, she ventures out with nothing but her name and a note of her last job...

Jackie unfortunately doesn't remember her past life before she was 'allegedly' shot in the head. There are rumors that the Khans and a man in a checkered suit tried to kill her, but she searches for him to get more answers than anything. Where did the chip she was delivering go? Did she know him before this? And why her specifically?

She hunts for answers first, and plans to decide from there. Although with each piece of new information, she wonders whether the truth will be worth it.
